Intermediate Level

  • This level focuses mainly on small mostly used kitchen objects.
  • To play pick 5 kitchen objects namely (a bucket, a plate, a bowl, a cup and a mug)
  • Place each of them infrom of the camera and pronounce the name written on the screen infront of the microphone.
  • If you get it right you will move to the next object if you don't, you'll try to pronounce again until you get it right.
